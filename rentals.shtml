Meetings and Special Event Rentals

Are you seeking that unique setting for your special event?

If you want to impress your guests with a memorable gathering in one of the most beautiful settings in Philadelphia, the Morris Arboretum's grounds and facilities will put your event in a class of its own, away from the rush of modern life.

Here you’ll find an enchanted ambiance with original Victorian architecture and fountains, colorfully landscaped gardens, and more than one hundred different tree specimens and eclectic landscaped gardens. For more information, contact the Rentals Team at 215.247.5777 x158 or email rentals@morrisarboretum.org.

Meetings

Our facility offers a variety of room combinations and set-ups available for meeting rentals. Quality audio-visual equipment can be provided for your use. During Café hours, food service is available through Company’s Coming. For food service at meetings occurring outside of Café hours, you may choose from any of our five approved caterers, who represent a variety of cuisine styles and price ranges.

Corporate Events/Special Events/Parties

Garden Rentals are available for special events from 6:00PM - 11:00PM. You may choose from a variety of outdoor settings -- each with its own enchanting backdrop and peaceful, natural surroundings for a memorable event. You and your party will have exclusive access to the grounds and the Widener Visitors Center carriage house. For more information, contact the Rentals Team at 215.247.5777 x158 or email rentals@morrisarboretum.org.
