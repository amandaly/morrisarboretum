Nonprofit Membership

Nonprofit Members enjoy the following benefits:

  • FREE unlimited admission for an entire year

  • Discounts at local garden centers & businesses
  • Complimentary one-time use guest passes
  • Discounts on educational classes, field trips and excursions(excludes professional training courses)
  • Invitationsto VIP events, plus Member’s Only Day at the Plant Sale

  • Print recognition in the Annual Report (Distribution: 6,000+)
  • Full-color Arboretum Calendar featuring photos, events and garden tips
