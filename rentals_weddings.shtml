Weddings at Morris Arboretum

Morris Arboretum is the perfect setting for a truly memorable wedding. Imagine saying "I do" among towering trees and lovely vistas. The grounds will be exclusively yours - all 92 acres of lush, romantic gardens, bubbling fountains, and historic features. Mother Nature provides the backdrop for a beautiful outdoor ceremony, while we provide a historic Carriage House with tented area for reception and friendly, knowledgeable staff.

Our extensive tree and plant collections ensure that the Morris Arboretum looks spectacular at any time of year, from the flowering magnolia and cherry trees in spring to breathtaking roses and wildflowers in summer. If you like cooler weather, our fall foliage can't be beat and, in winter, our variety of witchhazels, hollies, and large conifers really stand out in the cold.

Whether your party is small or large, your dream wedding is waiting for you at Morris Arboretum.

  • Morris Arboretum is available for weddings on Friday, Saturday and Sunday evenings, from 6:00pm to 11:00pm.
  • Morris Arboretum is conveniently located in Philadelphia's historic Northwest, just four miles from the Pennsylvania Turnpike and the Blue Route/I-476, and 20 minutes from Center City Philadelphia.
  • There are hotel accommodations nearby.
  • The use of the grounds of the Arboretum for wedding photography is limited to parties that have rented the facilities.
  • Morris Arboretum is a proud supporter of marriage equality.

For more information, contact Rentals and Information at 215.247.5777 x158 or email rentals@morrisarboretum.org.

Receptions

The Widener Visitors Center, an attractive former carriage house, serves as the base for your reception. (In case of inclement weather, the carriage house provides an indoor alternative for your ceremony. Your caterer is prepared to make the necessary transition flawlessly should the need arise).

Click here to see a list of our approved caterers.

  • With the use of the tented area attached to the Visitors Center, you can accommodate up to 175 guests for a sit-down reception (Tented area not available November through March).
  • The Upper Gallery of the building is suitable for hosting a smaller reception of up to 65 guests.
  • Weather permitting, the area in front of the Widener Visitors Center can serve as a lovely outdoor reception site for cocktails and hors d'oeuvres.
  • A bridal dressing area within the building provides a private, pleasant, comfortable retreat for the bride and attendants.
  • Restrooms are within this building and wheelchair accessible.
  • Use the beautiful gardens as a backdrop for your wedding photos.
  • There is convenient, free parking.
  • Tables, chairs, lights, side flaps and heaters (if needed) for the tented reception area.
